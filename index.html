Fast

This applications is so fast, you do not even need a connection! Yes, it works offline. It just one PWA (Progressive Web App) that behaves as a native app. It works as a charm in Android and iOS and you will not need to connect to the store - EVER - to get the last version. It will always be there for you.

Simple

Add items. Categorize them with tags. Filter and mark them as done. That's it. Create your list with your every day items, or items that you only buy once in a while. Tag them by store, department, dates or whatever fits you best. Select the store you are in and you will get all items to be purchased there.
